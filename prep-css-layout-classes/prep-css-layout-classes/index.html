MCU movies

Doctor Strange

In a long line of Marvel films, Doctor Strange comes with a story about an egotistical doctor humbled into searching for his own path in the mystical arts.

Spider-Man No Way Home

The newest release of the Marvel series, Spider-Man No Way Home opens the MCU to the concept of the multiverse and incorporates Sony's Spider-Man movies into the marvel continuum.

Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings

The first Asian American hero introduced into the MCU, Shang-Chi follows the story of a hero trained as an assassin since birth, searching for peace in his mother's footsteps.
